VA AUTONOMOUS SYSTEMS

VHA SimVET and VA Ventures under the Office of Healthcare Innovation and Learning (OHIL) is conducting research that aims to introduce robotic devices in VA healthcare and explore the role that AI-powered autonomous systems can play. Edmond Scientific is leading the requirements gathering to identify the best solutions and applications of autonomous systems and robots to support such areas as

  • Home health monitoring and compliance

  • Hospital distribution and delivery

  • Social interaction

  • Rehabilitation and physical therapy to support Veterans and caregivers at home and in their daily tasks.
